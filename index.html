Classic Syrnyky (Ukrainian Cottage Cheese Pancakes)

Ingredients

  • 400 g cottage cheese (firm, well-drained)
  • 1 egg
  • 2–3 tbsp sugar (to taste)
  • 1 tsp vanilla sugar (optional)
  • 3–4 tbsp all-purpose flour + some for dusting
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2–3 tbsp oil for frying

To Serve

  • Sour cream
  • Fresh blueberries (or other berries)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the cheese: If the cottage cheese is wet, squeeze it through cheesecloth. Mash it well or blend for a smoother texture.
  2. Mix the ingredients: Add the egg, sugar, vanilla sugar, salt, and flour to the cheese. Mix until a soft dough forms. It may be slightly sticky.
  3. Shape the syrnyky: Dust your hands and work surface with flour. Divide the dough into portions and form small round patties about 1.5–2 cm thick.
  4. Fry: Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Fry the syrnyky until golden brown on both sides, about 3–4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve: Place the warm syrnyky on a plate. Top with a spoonful of sour cream and fresh blueberries — just like in the picture.
